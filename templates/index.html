With Timefy you can easily order the songs in any of your playlists be a wide range of parameters. Just follow these steps:

  1. Login with your Spotify credentials
  2. Pick your playlist
  3. Configure your desired length and name it
  4. Save the sorted playlist to Spotify

FAQ

Here are some answers to questions about Timeify

  1. How was this built? This was created using Python, JS and the Spotify API.
  2. Is the source available - Yes, you can find it on Github
  3. Can I Contribute? - Of course! You can find the repo linked above
  4. Does Timeify overwrite my playlist when I save? - No, it creates a new playlist and saves it your library.
